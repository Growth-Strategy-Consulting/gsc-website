Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Growth Strategy Consulting ("GSC," "I," or "me") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information I collect through growthstrategyconsulting.org, how I use it, and the choices you have. Questions? Email hello@growthstrategyconsulting.org.

1. Information I collect

Information you give me

When you fill out a form, book a consultation, or email me, I collect details you choose to share, such as your name, email address, business name, industry, and anything you tell me about your business.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, mine uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site. This may include your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and how you found me. This is handled through HubSpot's analytics.

2. How I use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • To provide and improve my consulting services
  • To send you information or follow-ups you've requested or agreed to receive
  • To understand and improve how this website performs

I do not sell your personal information. Ever.

3. Cookies & tracking

My site uses cookies through HubSpot to recognize returning visitors and measure site activity. It also loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings, though some parts of the site may not work as well if you do.

4. Who I share information with

I share information only with trusted service providers that help me run my business:

  • HubSpot: customer relationship management, forms, scheduling, and email
  • Google Fonts: website typography
  • Netlify: website hosting

I may also disclose information if required by law. I never sell or rent your data.

5. Data retention

I keep your information for as long as needed to provide my services and stay in touch, or as required by law. You can ask me to delete it at any time (see "Your choices").

6. Your choices & rights

You can:

  • Ask what information I have about you
  • Ask me to correct or delete it
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time. Every email includes an unsubscribe link

To make any of these requests, just email hello@growthstrategyconsulting.org.

7. Email communications

If you opt in, I may send you occasional emails. You can unsubscribe anytime using the link in any email, and I'll honor it promptly.

8. Third-party links

My site may link to other websites, such as my booking page or social media. I'm not responsible for their privacy practices, so please review their policies.

9. Children's privacy

My site and services are intended for business owners and are not directed to children under 16. I don't knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

I may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email hello@growthstrategyconsulting.org.
