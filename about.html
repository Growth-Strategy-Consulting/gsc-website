Hi, I'm Elena.

I started Growth Strategy Consulting because too many hard-working business owners hit the same wall: the business that used to feel exciting now feels heavy, and no one's telling them the truth about why.

Over eight hands-on turnarounds—cafés, boutique hotels, plant shops, IT firms, even film sets—I've walked in, looked past the surface, and found what was quietly draining time, profit, and energy. Then I built the systems, processes, and structure to fix it for good.

I'm direct, I'm practical, and I don't do get-rich-quick. What I do is sit beside you, tell you the truth, and stay in it until the changes actually stick. Some clients I've now walked with for years—building their brand and growing it season after season.

— Elena
