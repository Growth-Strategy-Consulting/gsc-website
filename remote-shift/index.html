The Shift

It was never about the moon.
It's about becoming a woman who knows her worth.

For years I read every room so hard I forgot to ask what I wanted.

Then one day it hit me: nobody else is thinking about the things I tortured myself over. We're our own worst critics — everyone else is too busy starring in their own life to be writing yours. So I let it go. I stopped being a side character in mine.

I decided who I wanted to become, then taught myself every skill it took to get there. Built a life that runs on my design — the kind I'll look back on and be glad I didn't wait to start.

The Remote Shift is everything I've learned, in one place, handed to you.

— Elena
